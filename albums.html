Juice WRLD's Most Famous Albums

From heartbreak to hip-hop stardom — the story of a legend gone too soon. Discover the life of Juice WRLD, a voice that changed a generation.

Goodbye & Good Riddance (2018)

Goodbye & Good Riddance (2018) Juice WRLD’s debut studio album that launched him into mainstream success. Featuring the massive hit “Lucid Dreams,” this project deals with heartbreak, addiction, and emotional vulnerability. It introduced his unique sound—melancholic, melodic, and raw. Notable tracks: Lucid Dreams, All Girls Are the Same, Lean Wit Me, Wasted

Legends Never Die (2020, posthumous)

Released after Juice WRLD’s tragic death in 2019, this posthumous album became one of the most successful releases of 2020. It’s a heartfelt tribute to his legacy, filled with reflections on fame, pain, mental health, and hope. The album broke several streaming records and cemented his influence on a generation.
